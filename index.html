Welcome to a modular SolidOS front-end. Take it for a spin, being sure to notice that the center menu relates to the person you are visiting while the left-hand menu (after logging in, click the ≡) relates to you. Visit a friend and see!

Then look at view source of this page. You'll see this is a zero-code app. Everything is loaded using simple HTML tags. To find out more check out the WiP Solid UIX repo.
